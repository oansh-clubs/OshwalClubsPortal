    Media Hub


    Welcome to the vibrant hub of creativity and expression - Our Oshwal Academy Nairobi Senior High's Media Hub! As a diverse community of budding programmers, writers, photographers, and gamers, we come together to explore the vast and ever-evolving world of media. Dive into our virtual space where pixels meet passion, words dance on screens, and every frame tells a unique story. We host many events and competitions throughout the year from photography to programming and gaming.

    Mission Statement

    Empowering creativity and diversity through media exploration.


    Vision Statement

    To be a dynamic hub inspiring transformative experiences and fostering the next generation of media enthusiasts.


    Goals

    Our goals at the Media Hub are to foster creativity through diverse media forms like photography and videography, to develop technical skills in production and editing, and to cultivate a collaborative environment where members can share ideas and work on projects together. We aim to engage the school community through events showcasing our members' talents, provide resources for career exploration in media fields, and support personal growth and leadership among our members. Join us as we explore, create, and inspire through the power of media.
